# 事项跟踪单

Use this reference when the user asks for 事项跟踪单, 事项跟踪表, 问题整改跟踪, 工作事项闭环, 督办事项, or similar fixed-format tracking documents.

## Goal

Turn scattered notes into a structured tracking document that makes each matter easy to assign, follow, update, and close.

## Recommended Structure

Use a table when possible.

| 序号 | 事项名称 | 背景/来源 | 当前进展 | 存在问题 | 下一步措施 | 责任人/责任部门 | 完成时限 | 状态 | 备注 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

If the user wants a narrative version, use:

1. 基本情况
2. 事项清单
3. 存在问题
4. 下一步安排
5. 需协调事项

## Field Guidance

- 事项名称: short noun phrase, not a long sentence.
- 背景/来源: where the matter came from, such as meeting decision,领导交办,检查发现,基层反馈.
- 当前进展: completed facts only.
- 存在问题: blockers, risks, unclear ownership, missing inputs.
- 下一步措施: concrete action, not a slogan.
- 责任人/责任部门: preserve exact names if provided; otherwise use placeholder.
- 完成时限: preserve exact date if provided; otherwise use `【完成时限】`.
- 状态: choose from `未启动`, `推进中`, `需协调`, `已完成`, `已关闭`.

## Writing Style

- Use short, action-oriented language.
- Keep one matter per row.
- Do not bury deadlines in long paragraphs.
- Keep "问题" and "措施" separate.
- Use `需协调事项` for blockers that need leadership or cross-department support.

## Minimal Text Template

标题：事项跟踪单

一、基本情况
简要说明事项来源、跟踪范围和总体进展。

二、事项推进情况
按表格列明事项名称、当前进展、存在问题、下一步措施、责任人和完成时限。

三、需协调解决事项
列明需要协调的事项、建议协调方式和建议责任主体。

四、下一步工作安排
按时间顺序列明近期重点动作。
